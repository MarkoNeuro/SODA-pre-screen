Welcome to the SODA Study

Thank you for your interest in participating in the SODA study. You are reading this because you are interested in contributing to groundbreaking research on decision-making and anxiety.

The SODA study explores how certain treatments influence decision-making processes in individuals with high levels of anxiety. This research aims to deepen our understanding of these effects to improve future treatments.

Before proceeding, please review the Participant Information Sheet for important details about the study, including its purpose, procedures, and your rights as a participant.

To determine your eligibility for the study, you will need to answer a few prescreening questions. This process will take only a few minutes.
